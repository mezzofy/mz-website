### SECTION 5 — How It Works
*(White background, horizontal 4-step flow, numbered circles like the merchant journey section)*

**Heading:** From Merchant Listing to AI Redemption in Four Steps

**Step 1 — LIST**
- Description: Merchant lists coupon inventory in the Mezzofy Exchange via the self-serve supply portal. Zero upfront cost.

**Step 2 — INDEX**
- Description: Mezzofy tokenizes and standardizes inventory, then exposes it via ACP, UCP, and MCP protocol endpoints. Machine-readable, fraud-proof.

**Step 3 — DISCOVER**
- Description: AI agents query Mezzofy supply in real time when users ask for deals. Matched coupons surface inside ChatGPT, Gemini, Kimi, or partner apps.

**Step 4 — REDEEM**
- Description: Consumer redeems directly within the AI interface or partner app. Mezzofy validates the token, settles with the merchant, and earns a transaction fee.
