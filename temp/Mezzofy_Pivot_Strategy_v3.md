## NFC REPOSITIONED: FROM MERCHANT PRODUCT TO DISTRIBUTION NETWORK

### BEFORE · Current Model
- Merchant pays $79/month for NFC tag
- Tag only works for that one merchant's coupons
- If merchant churns → tag goes dead
- Sales cycle: sell each merchant individually
- Value: limited to their own foot traffic only
- No network effect when adding more merchants

### AFTER · Channel Distribution Node
- Channel partner (mall/HR) owns & deploys the tag
- Any merchant in the supply pool flows through it
- If one merchant leaves, another replaces them — tag stays alive
- One channel partner deal = many merchants activated
- Value: access to captive audience (office workers, shoppers)
- More channel nodes = more distribution for all merchants

## BLOCKCHAIN BUSINESS OPPORTUNITIES: COUPONS AS TOKENIZED ASSETS

RWA tokenization market: $33B today → projected $10 trillion by 2030. Mezzofy's tokenized coupon infrastructure is already on-chain architecture. The question is not if — it's how far we take it.

### Foundation · What We Already Have

**Coupon Tokens as RWA** — Each Mezzofy coupon is already a tokenized, single-use, fraud-proof digital asset. This is the on-chain primitive for a broader promotional asset market. Merchants issue coupon tokens with defined redemption rules, expiry, and value — exactly how RWA instruments are structured. *Mezzofy coupons ARE real-world assets. The infrastructure already exists.*

**Smart Contract Redemption** — Replace current clearinghouse logic with smart contracts. Redemption triggers automatic settlement to merchant wallets. No manual reconciliation. No payment delays. Programmable conditions: "Pay merchant only if coupon redeemed between 2–5pm on weekdays." *Eliminates reconciliation overhead. Enables real-time merchant payouts.*

**Cross-Chain Coupon Portability** — A tokenized Mezzofy coupon earned via SCB's loyalty program could be transferred to a China Mobile wallet and redeemed at a Grab-partnered merchant. Blockchain interoperability (via protocols like Chainlink CCIP) enables the first truly cross-platform coupon standard. *First mover opportunity: the universal coupon token standard across Asian markets.*

### Frontier · Where We Can Go

**Coupon DeFi: Yield on Unsold Inventory** — Merchants with unused coupon inventory can stake tokens in a liquidity pool. Distribution partners (banks, telcos) earn yield by committing to distribute. Creates a financial incentive layer around promotional inventory. *New revenue stream: Mezzofy earns protocol fees on liquidity pool activity.*

**NFT Loyalty Collectibles** — Premium coupons issued as limited NFTs — "First 100 customers at new restaurant opening". Creates scarcity, collectability, and secondary market value. Integrates naturally with bank rewards and telco loyalty tiers. *Visa, Lufthansa, and Salesforce already piloting NFT loyalty programs globally.*

**Tokenized Coupon Marketplace (Secondary)** — Allow users to trade unwanted coupons on a secondary market. "I have a spa voucher but want a dining voucher." Peer-to-peer coupon exchange with Mezzofy earning a marketplace fee on every trade. *Unlocks liquidity in the $16T global coupon market. Entirely new business model.*
