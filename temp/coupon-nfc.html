Frequently Asked Questions

How long does account activation take?

Account activation typically takes 24-48 hours after signup. Once activated, you can immediately start creating and distributing coupons through your NFC tags.

How are coupons counted?

Each unique coupon instance distributed to a customer counts toward your monthly or annual limit. Multiple taps by the same customer for the same coupon only count once.

Can I modify offers after distribution?

Yes! Our platform allows real-time modifications to active campaigns. You can update offer terms, extend expiration dates, void coupons, or refine targeting parameters at any time through the dashboard.

What happens if I exceed my coupon limit?

If you approach your coupon limit, we'll notify you in advance. You can either upgrade to a higher plan or purchase additional coupon credits. There's no service interruption - excess usage is billed at a competitive per-coupon rate.
